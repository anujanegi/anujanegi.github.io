Anuja Negi


I'm a masters student at the Bernstein Center for Computational Neuroscience, Berlin, pursuing a joint degree from the Technische Universität Berlin (TU Berlin) and the Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin (HU Berlin). Before that, I got my Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering from VIT, India.

I work on machine learning, deep learning and computational neuroscience. I'm especially interested in understanding the computational foundations of natural intelligence.
